🚢 BONDED TERMINAL CLEARING FLOW (REAL OPERATION)
🟢 STAGE 1: JOB INTAKE (REGISTER CONTAINER)
What happens:
Client gives you:
B/L (Bill of Lading)
Invoice
Packing List
What you do:
Open job in system
Create container record
Assign staff
Stage Owner:

👉 Documentation / Ops

🟡 STAGE 2: DOCUMENTATION & PAAR PROCESS
What happens:
Prepare all customs documents
Includes:
Form M validation
PAAR processing
SON / NAFDAC (if needed)
Goal:

👉 Get PAAR BEFORE vessel arrival

Stage Owner:

👉 Documentation Team

🔵 STAGE 3: DUTY ASSESSMENT (SGD / DECLARATION)
What happens:
Customs calculates duty
What you do:
Check assessment
Confirm duty amount
Stage Owner:

👉 Documentation / Customs Desk

🟠 STAGE 4: DUTY PAYMENT
What happens:
Duty must be paid
What you do:
Collect funds OR use company funds
Pay through bank
⚠️ IMPORTANT:

👉 This must be done before or immediately after arrival

Stage Owner:

👉 Accounts

🔴 STAGE 5: TRANSIRE PROCESS (CORE OF BONDED TERMINAL)
What happens:
Container is approved to move from port → bonded terminal
What you do:
Apply for transire
Get customs approval
⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT:

👉 Without transire = no movement

Stage Owner:

👉 Operations / Transire Team

🟣 STAGE 6: SHIPPING LINE & TERMINAL PAYMENTS
What happens:
Pay:
Shipping line charges
Terminal charges
Obtain:
Delivery Order (DO / TDO)
What you do:
Pay debit note
Secure release documents
Stage Owner:

👉 Accounts + Ops

🟤 STAGE 7: PULL-OUT FROM PORT
What happens:
Container leaves seaport to bonded terminal
What you do:
Assign truck
Process gate-out
Move container
Timing:

👉 Usually Day 2–3 after arrival

Stage Owner:

👉 Transport / Ops

⚫ STAGE 8: ARRIVAL AT BONDED TERMINAL (GATE-IN)
What happens:
Container enters your bonded terminal
What you do:
Record arrival
Prepare for examination
Stage Owner:

👉 Terminal Team

⚪ STAGE 9: CUSTOMS EXAMINATION (AT BONDED TERMINAL)
What happens:
Physical inspection happens at YOUR terminal
What you do:
Arrange customs inspection
Present goods
Resolve any issues
⚠️ KEY DIFFERENCE:

👉 In bonded terminal, examination happens HERE (not main port)

Stage Owner:

👉 Operations

🟢 STAGE 10: FINAL CUSTOMS RELEASE
What happens:
Customs gives final clearance
What you do:
Secure release approval
Complete documentation
Stage Owner:

👉 Operations / Customs Desk

🚚 STAGE 11: DELIVERY TO CLIENT
What happens:
Container is delivered to customer
What you do:
Assign truck
Deliver goods
Confirm delivery
Timing:

👉 Must be FAST to avoid extra cost

Stage Owner:

👉 Transport Team

🔄 STAGE 12: EMPTY RETURN
What happens:
Empty container returned to shipping line
What you do:
Arrange return immediately
Avoid demurrage
⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT:

👉 Delay here = extra charges

Stage Owner:

👉 Transport

🧾 STAGE 13: JOB CLOSURE
What happens:
Close the job financially and operationally
What you do:
Confirm payments
Issue final invoice
Close container
Stage Owner:

👉 Accounts / Admin

🧠 SIMPLE FLOW (BONDED TERMINAL)

👉 In one clean line:

Register → Documentation → Duty Assessment → Duty Payment → Transire → Pay Shipping/Terminal → Pull-Out → Gate-In (Bonded Terminal) → Examination → Final Release → Delivery → Empty Return → Close

🔥 KEY DIFFERENCE (VERY IMPORTANT)

Compared to normal clearing:

👉 BONDED TERMINAL adds:

Transire (movement approval)
Gate-in at bonded terminal
Examination at bonded terminal

Registered
