1. FIXED PIPELINE FOR YOUR APP

Your current pipeline is good, but it needs to reflect bonded terminal reality more accurately. Right now, stages like “Customs Examination” and “Terminal Processing” are a bit too broad for the bonded-terminal flow described in your strategic document and system spec.

Recommended bonded-terminal pipeline
1) Registered

Container has been created in the system.

2) Documentation

B/L, invoice, packing list, Form M, PAAR prep, and related document checks are in progress.

3) Duty Assessment

Assessment/SGD/declaration is being processed and duty amount is being confirmed.

4) Duty Payment

Duty is ready and must be paid or has just been paid.

5) Transire Processing

Transire application and customs approval to move to bonded terminal.

6) Shipping / Terminal Payment

Shipping line and terminal debit note payments, DO/TDO release process.

7) Pull-Out

Container is being pulled out from port and assigned to a truck.

8) Gate-In at Bonded Terminal

Container has arrived at your bonded terminal.

9) Examination

Physical customs examination at bonded terminal.

10) Final Release

Final customs release/approval after examination.

11) Delivery

Truck assigned, container moved to client destination, delivery in progress or completed.

12) Empty Return

Empty container return process to shipping line/terminal.

13) Closed

Job fully completed and closed financially/operationally.

Mapping from your current pipeline to the corrected one

Your current documented stages are: Registered, Documentation, Customs Examination, Duty Assessment, Duty Payment, Terminal Processing, Awaiting Delivery, In Transit, Completed, Closed.

Here is the better mapping:

Registered → keep
Documentation → keep
Customs Examination → move later and rename to Examination
Duty Assessment → keep
Duty Payment → keep
Terminal Processing → split into:
Transire Processing
Shipping / Terminal Payment
Pull-Out
Gate-In at Bonded Terminal
Final Release
Awaiting Delivery + In Transit + Completed → replace with:
Delivery
Empty Return
Closed

So the biggest correction is this:

👉 Your current “Terminal Processing” stage is carrying too much work. It should be broken into separate operational stages so delays can be traced properly.
